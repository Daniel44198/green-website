🌱 Green Living

Small actions make a big difference

What is sustainability?

Sustainability means living in a way that does not harm the planet, reducing consumption and protecting natural resources.

Current Problems

  • Pollution
  • Food waste
  • Excessive use of plastic

Solutions

  • Recycle
  • Reduce consumption
  • Use renewable energy

How to Help

You can start with small actions like reusing items, avoiding waste, and consuming responsibly.
